The Point at Schroon Lake

A private retreat available to rent throughout the year.

A Grand Adirondack Escape

Our grand lakefront property, centrally set on pristine lakefront, offers some of the most magnificent views on Schroon Lake, as it overlooks the entire lake and the mountains beyond. With plenty of room to comfortably house 22 individuals in the Lakefront Lodge and up to 8 in the Hilltop Retreat; our homes are ideal for families, friends, and large groups. It’s the perfect Schroon Lake vacation rental year round, just a 5 minute drive from I-87 NYS Thruway and the charming village of Schroon Lake.

Explore The Area

Explore trails and attractions in the following communities:

  • Newcomb, NY
  • Keene Valley, NY
  • Ticonderoga, NY
  • Saranac Lake, NY
  • Tupper Lake, NY
  • Blue Mountain Lake, NY
  • Lake George, NY
  • Lake Placid, NY
A 5-minute drive in either direction can put you on the Thruway heading to a new destination, or it can put you in the quaint little village of Schroon Lake. The village offers multiple shops and eateries, plus numerous local events, festivals and activities year-round. If you’re in for a little more adventure, take a ride to the high peaks wilderness just 45 minutes away. There you can enjoy world-class hiking and recreation, as well as the renowned city of Lake Placid. Whiteface Ski Center and many of the other Olympic Venues are just a short drive away. If you’re a total history buff, Fort Ticonderoga and Fort William Henry are both within an hour drive of our location. If you’re in the mood for a little evening entertainment, you can stop by the award-winning Paradox Brewery just 10 minutes down the road.
